Nobodyelses Finance is a model-first stock screener.

Create your own custom formulas with over 20-years of historical data, or use ours.

Create custom formulas

Create custom formulas just as you would in a spreadsheet, but this "spreadsheet" has over 20-years of historical data, for every company, already loaded. Leverage built in functions such as AVERAGE, GROWTH, IRR, etc., plus you can create your own functions too.

Adjust financial statements

Ratios used for screening are only as good as the data used to calculate them. Adjust company financial statements to your personal preference. Adjustments flow through the end of the statement and are reflected in all calculations on the company report immediately.

Model the entire market

Why screen to reduce the universe of stocks down to a manageable few when you can model the entire market first with custom formulas, then screen using good-better-best ranges, multiple column sorts, and weighted filters. Get what you want in the order you want it.
